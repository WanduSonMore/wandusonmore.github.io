Visions of Murder

Summary:

Visions of Murder is a first person horror game where you play as detective Steven Kirkman, a gruff man in his early thirties. Kirkman has taken on a case involving a series of strange murders and the police have not found anyway to track down the murder. Previously Kirkman had taken on a case that ended in a innocent person dying that haunted him and his dreams. After taking on this new case his nightmares start showing things in current case. In the dreams Kirkman sees through the eyes of the victims in the murders and witness them being murdered by some sort of supernatural entity. At first Kirkman believes that they are just dreams, but when his dreams start predicting showing how the murders are being commited before the body is found, he realizes there is more to these than he thought. Using his dreams as his guide he tries to stop the murder, he realizes that he may be involved in some sort of conflict between two powerful entities. One being the murder and the other being the entity who is giving him these visions in order to stop the first. Will Kirkman be able to stop this supernatural murder or will he die like the victims in his dreams?

Story Line:

Prolog - In this section player plays through a scene from the case that haunted Kirkman where we learn why that case haunted him.

1. They pull up to build with suspect they are after.

2. Enter build and find the suspect hold a hostage.

3. Suspect kills hostage and is gunned down by Kirkman.

4. Kirkman goes to victim side to see if they are alright only to be jumpscared by the victim grabbing him and saying, "Why didn't you save me." Kirkman then wakes up from this revealing to the player that the whole scene was a dream and leads into the beginning section.

Beginning - This is where the intro credits role and the scenes involving Kirkman first taking on the case for the game.

1. Kirkman wakes up, gets ready for the day, looks at himself in the mirror, and drives to police precinct.

2. Talks to his partner Wade Gillfree, gets his case, and views a couple of pictures from the case.

3. Checks out the most recent murder scene and sees some strange things like a metal door that was ripped apart like it was paper. Crazy homeless guy they find says that it was a monster who killed the victim.

4. Kirkman goes home and goes to bed.

Dream 1 - This dream Kirkman is seeing through the eyes of a woman by the name of Andrea and watches as she is trying to get away from something from in a old warehouse.

1. Andrea wakes up inside a warehouse in the middle of the night after being knock unconcious. Also all the doors to get out of the building are locked.

2. Andrea is chased arounnd the warehouse by a monster try

Dream 2 - This dream Kirkman is seeing through the eyes of a man by the name of Hank and watches as he is trying to get away from someone from in a forest.

1. Hank wakes up in the middle of a old kids cabin camp that was closed for the fall. Hank is injured and he doesn't know who attacked him and brought him here.

2. Hank wonders around the area while he feels like he is being stalked some sort of monster and is attacked by said monster.

3. Hanks spends the time trying to find a way to contact the police. When he gets to a phone he is attack and killed. Kirkman wakes up from the dream.

Waking 2 - This is where Kirkman goes with his partner to check a body that was found in a kids camp that matches the murders previous kills.
