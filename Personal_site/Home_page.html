VISIONS OF MURDER

Game Category: Horror, Mystery

Description: This is a first-person horror game where you play as a detective who takes on a case involving a string of murders. During his investigation, he starts having visions about the murders through the eyes of the victims. These visions show that these murders are being committed by some sort of monster, but they can't be real right?

THE ENDLESS HOUSE

Game Category: Horror

Description: This is a third person horror game where you play as a young college student named Heather Shields, who went with her two friends to explore an old abandon manor house who everyone says is haunted. It is only after entering that they realized that the manor is not haunted house but is far more dangerous.
