THE ENDLESS HOUSE

Summary:

The Endless House is a first person horror game where you play as a young half black woman named Heather Shields, who is attending college in the year 1984 she is good at history, archery, and her dad taught her how to shoot. After a long year Heather has finished up this years class and celebrates it with two of her friends, Aaron and Jackson. They were all into stuff about ghosts and haunted places, so they decide to celebrate the end of term by checking out an old abandon manor house that Jackson heard about, but after getting there and spending some time in the manor it becomes very clear that there is something far more sinister going on in this house than just your average haunting. When trying to leave the house they find the door they come though gone and they are attack by monsters and insane people. After being seperated from her friends she is saved by a man named Noah Becker, who had been studying the anomolies in the house with his now deceased partner. He had been in the house for six months and has been trying to stop the evil entity that controls this endless building. With the help of Noah, Heather is then tasked with destroy three condoits that give the house power and allow manipulate reality within the house, which helps the house keep anyone who comes into the building trapped in it and prevents them from finding and destroying the object or heart that gives it life. Only by destroying these condoits can Heather and Noah gain access to the houses heart and destroy it, which would allow them to go free.

Story Line:

Beginning - Player watches as finishes her last test and heads off on her bike to hang out with her friends.

1. Intro credits

2. Character introduction

3. Plan to checkout an old abandon manor house

Into the endless house - This is where they first enter the house

1. They get to the house and find the front door unlocked and go inside to explore the place.

2. Start hearing strange screeches coming towards them, so they run for the door only to find it gone.

3. they get attack by a huge man (The Thug) with a spiked club for an arm and a executioners hood. This leads to Heather getting seperated from her friends.

The Rescue - Heather is about to be killed by the thug when she is pulled through a door by Noah.

1. Noah saves Heather by pulling her into a room that was reconstruct in a way that allowed Noah to make the room move, teleport around, and be unable to be altered by the house. He dubs it the safe room.

2. Noah explains how the house works and how to stop it by destroying the living conduits that gave the house power. He also gives her a makeshift bow and arrows that he has made to defend himself.

The first conduit - In this section Heather goes after the first conduit, who is a small violent man by the name of Gabe who is from 1929 during the great depression and is the brother of the thug monster named York that attacked heather in the beginning. The thug is dumb and alway followed his weaker, but smarter older brother.

1. This is the section where the player will first meet the hollows which are zombies like monsters that the play can choose to kill or avoid.

2. In this section Heather will meet up with Aaron again.

3. the player will mainly spends this section trying to avoid and hide from York, who had been ordered to kill the player by Gabe. Player will find it impossible to kill him and thus will try run away from and avoid him, but eventually Heather with the help of Aaron defeats York by making him fall off a balcony where he falls and dies. Aaron's leg is injured in this encounter.

4. Heather finds Gade who begs for his life, but ultimately he is killed by Heather. Noah helps Heather and Aaron into the safe room.

The second conduit - In this section heather goes after the second conduit, who is a beautiful looking but dangerous woman named Lira. She is from the 1950's and may have been a little know Hollywood star. She is very dangerous and is able to hypnotize men to do her bidding and has a particular haterid for women who get to close to her pets. In addition to hypnosis she can use a blade like scarf to harm the player.

1. In this section Heather finds Jackson, but it is revealed that he is being mind controlled by her. He tricks her into a trap where she meets Lira and is then thrown into a hole that leads to a wine cellar. In the cellar she is attacked by her less favored puppets.

2. The player spends most of their time fighting the puppets off and avoiding the. Eventually they will be forced to fight Jackson and knock him out.

3. This section ends with the player confronting Lira and battling with her, until she is shot in the heart by the player and is defeated. Noah helps Heather get Jackson into the safe room.

The third conduit - In this section the player goes after the third conduit, who is thought to be a slave owner named Harvey Macrow from the 1850's, but is later revealed to actually be his son John Macrow. Harvey has been using his son's conduit abilities to avoid death and become very powerful, this made it look like he was the conduit. He believes that his son is a disgrace because he fell in love with in love with a black servent girl named Hana, but he doesn't remember what Hana actually looks like due to the son trying to protect Hana, who is still in the house, from the wrath of his. This effect on him also drove him to insanity.

1. Hana travels through this area when she is captured by the slave hunters (Strange emotionless men with pits for eyes), has her weapons taken from her and is brought before Harvey who is being servered by a african American girl and has his son sitting next to him tied up. At seeing her, he believes that Heather is one of his slaves and believe that she was trying to run away from him. He orders that she should be thrown into solitary confinement and the slave hunters take her to a dank basement.

2. The player will eventually find a old tunnel that leads up stairs. They will then have to sneak past the slave hunter, find a knife and attempts to kill Harvey. This fails and Heather is caught again. She is brought to Harvey again, who doesn't even realise that this is the same person he sent to solitary confinement. instead upon hearing that she was try to kill him believes that she is Hana because that is the only explination as to why she would try to kill him. Also remember that he is crazy.

3. He decides to play a game with the player by making them have to run for their life through the house while being chased by the slave hunters. Heather is chase through the house and gets corner at a locked door at the end of a hall, but suddenly someone opens the locked door and pulls her through before she is seen by the hunters. The player will find that the person who saved them is the same African American woman Heather saw serving Harvey earlier.

4. The maid reveals to the player that she is Hana and also tells the player that Harvey is not the conduit, but his son John. It is also revealed that it was Harvey who brought the object that made the endless house. Hana gives back Heather's weapons. Hana ask the player to kill Harvey and to free John from his suffering. Heather agrees to help her and free John

5. Heather avoids the hunters and makes to Harvey. She fights him and defeats him. She then sets John free from this hollow life.

Ending - This is when the house begins to fall apart and become weakened enough for Noah to warp the safe room closer to the heart of the house. Heather goes into a room with a strange object in the middle of the room, which she picks it up and then smashes it. This ends the game.
